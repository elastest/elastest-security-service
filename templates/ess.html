    How to use?

    • 1. Go to the "Scan Configuration" section, remove the domains you want to exclude from scanning and click the "START SCAN" button
    • 2. Go the the "Scan Progress & Report" section and visualize the progress of the scan
    • 3. Notice each security issue displayed and make adequate changes in the SuT to resolve the issues based on their severity
    • 4. Re-run the TJob to re-scan the SuT and check whether the resolved security issues are not reported again
